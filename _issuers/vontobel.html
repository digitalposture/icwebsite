{{ page.title }} is a Swiss investment house and one of Europe’s leading issuers of structured products, operating globally from its headquarters in Zurich. Through its Structured Investment Solutions (SIS) division, Vontobel engineers, prices and distributes a wide range of investment and leverage products, including tracker certificates, bonus structures, reverse convertibles, capital protection notes and mini futures. The SIS business has shown strong momentum, with operating income rising significantly and Vontobel maintaining its position as the number one issuer on the SIX Swiss Exchange’s structured‑products segment. The bank issued more than 40,000 listed products in the public market, supported by deep derivatives expertise, robust risk management and a focus on high‑quality manufacturing. With a heritage dating back to 1936 and a global presence across Europe, the US, Asia and the Middle East, Vontobel combines long‑term stability with product innovation, making it a key issuer for investors seeking transparent, exchange‑listed structured solutions.
