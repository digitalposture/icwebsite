{{ page.title }} are structured investment products issued by Mediobanca – Banca di Credito Finanziario S.p.A., designed to offer investors tailored exposure to equity indices, single stocks and other underlyings with defined payoff profiles. Through ranges such as Bonus, Phoenix, Express, Twin Win and Protection, Mediobanca Certificates combine conditional capital protection, potential coupons and diversified risk/return profiles, making them suitable for different market views (bullish, neutral or moderately bearish). The certificates are listed primarily on EuroTLX (segment Cert-X) and other Italian trading venues, providing secondary market liquidity and transparent pricing. Each product is governed by a base prospectus and final terms, with key information documents (KIDs) detailing risks, costs and performance scenarios, allowing retail and professional investors to compare structures and align them with their portfolio objectives.
