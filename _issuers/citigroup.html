{{ page.title }} is a leading global financial institution headquartered in New York, operating one of the world’s most extensive international banking networks across nearly 160 countries and jurisdictions. Through its two core divisions — Institutional Clients Group (ICG) and Personal Banking & Wealth Management (PBWM) — Citi provides corporate banking, markets, treasury services, wealth management, and consumer finance solutions to multinational corporations, financial institutions, governments, and private clients. Citi’s Markets & Securities Services business is a major participant in global fixed income, equities, commodities, and derivatives, supported by deep liquidity, long-standing market-making capabilities, and a broad global presence. Citigroup’s structured products activity is primarily conducted through Citigroup Global Markets Holdings Inc. and Citigroup Global Markets Inc., which issue a wide range of investment products including notes, certificates, and warrants. These instruments are typically backed by hedging arrangements with Citigroup affiliates, leveraging the Group’s strong balance sheet, diversified revenue base, and robust risk-management framework. With a history spanning over 200 years, consistent capital strength, and a central role in global financial markets, Citigroup remains a key issuer for investors seeking international reach, product innovation, and institutional-grade stability.
