{{ page.title }} is a Swiss fintech and specialist provider of structured investment solutions, headquartered in Zurich and operating across 12 countries in Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. The company combines a proprietary technology platform with deep derivatives expertise to manufacture, distribute, and manage a broad universe of structured products for institutional and professional investors. Leonteq acts both as a direct issuer and as a white-labelling partner for banks and insurance companies, enabling them to produce capital-efficient, unit-linked pension and savings products with embedded guarantees. Its marketplace connects over 25 third-party issuers and offers more than 90 payoff types across thousands of underlyings, supported by automated pricing, risk management, and lifecycle servicing. Listed on the SIX Swiss Exchange (ticker: LEON), Leonteq maintains an investment-grade rating and continues to expand its international footprint through Leonteq Europe and additional offices in Asia and the Middle East.
