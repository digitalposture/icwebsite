{{ page.title }} is one of Europe’s largest and most diversified financial institutions, operating across corporate and institutional banking, retail banking, and investment & protection services. Headquartered in Paris and present in more than 65 countries, the Group combines global scale with deep regional roots across France, Belgium, Italy, Luxembourg, and the broader EMEA region. Its Corporate & Institutional Banking division is a leading provider of global markets, financing, and securities services, while its retail and specialized businesses — including BNP Paribas Personal Finance, Arval, and Leasing Solutions — serve millions of clients across Europe and beyond. Through BNP Paribas Issuance B.V., the Group acts as a major issuer of structured products, including certificates, notes, and warrants, distributed to private and institutional investors worldwide. The issuance vehicle is backed by BNP Paribas S.A. through robust hedging arrangements designed to manage market, credit, and operational risks associated with structured product activity. With strong capitalization, consistent profitability, and a long-standing presence in international financial markets, BNP Paribas remains a reference issuer for investors seeking stability, liquidity, and product innovation.
