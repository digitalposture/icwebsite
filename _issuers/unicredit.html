{{ page.title }} is a leading pan-European banking group headquartered in Milan, operating across Italy, Germany, Austria and Central & Eastern Europe. Through its Markets and Investment Products divisions, UniCredit is a major issuer of structured investment products, offering certificates and notes across a wide range of payoff types including Bonus, Cash Collect, Express, Protection and thematic tracker structures. UniCredit Certificates are listed primarily on Borsa Italiana’s SeDeX and EuroTLX markets, providing transparent pricing, secondary‑market liquidity and access for both retail and professional investors. The bank leverages its strong balance sheet, diversified geographic footprint and long-standing expertise in equity, rates and credit derivatives to manufacture products with defined risk/return profiles. Supported by detailed prospectuses, KIDs and lifecycle servicing, UniCredit remains one of Europe’s most active issuers, known for product innovation, broad underlying selection and robust risk management.
