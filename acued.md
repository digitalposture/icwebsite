# ACUED Scoring Framework  
Accuracy • Clarity • User Experience • Exhaustiveness • Discoverability

A modern evaluation model for structured‑product pages, underlying‑quote pages, and issuer ecosystems.

---

## 1. A — Data Reliability  
How trustworthy, timely, and accurate the displayed data is.

Criteria  
- Real‑time or near‑real‑time quotes  
- Clear timestamping  
- Verified coupon dates & event history  
- Consistency with official documents  
- No stale or contradictory data  

Score Guide  
- 1 — Outdated or inconsistent  
- 2 — Partially updated, unreliable  
- 3 — Mostly correct, occasional gaps  
- 4 — Reliable, well‑timed, clearly sourced  
- 5 — Fully verified, real‑time, audit‑ready  

---

## 2. C — Clarity (Information Transparency)  
How easily users understand payoff mechanics, coupon schedules, barriers, and P&L scenarios.

Criteria  
- Plain‑language payoff explanation  
- Visual payoff diagrams  
- Clear coupon calendar  
- P&L scenarios or payoff simulator  
- Highlighted key risks  

Score Guide  
- 1 — Confusing, jargon‑heavy  
- 2 — Basic info, unclear structure  
- 3 — Understandable but fragmented  
- 4 — Clear, well‑structured, visual  
- 5 — Crystal‑clear, interactive, intuitive  

---

## 3. U — User Experience (UX)  
How easy and pleasant the page is to use.

Criteria  
- Clean layout  
- Logical grouping of sections  
- Smooth navigation  
- Mobile optimization  
- Fast loading  

Score Guide  
- 1 — Hard to navigate  
- 2 — Usable but clunky  
- 3 — Decent UX with minor friction  
- 4 — Smooth, intuitive, consistent  
- 5 — Premium UX, frictionless  

---

## 4. E — Exhaustiveness (Content Completeness)  
Whether the page provides all essential information about the certificate and its underlyings.

Criteria  
- Payoff type  
- Barrier levels  
- Coupon history  
- Memory status  
- Underlying fundamentals  
- Lifecycle events  
- Documents (KID, Final Terms, Prospectus)  

Score Guide  
- 1 — Major gaps  
- 2 — Partial info  
- 3 — Most essentials present  
- 4 — Very complete  
- 5 — Fully comprehensive, no external sources needed  

---

## 5. D — Discoverability  
How easily users can find related certificates, underlyings, themes, or product categories.

Criteria  
- Suggested similar certificates  
- Underlying‑linked product lists  
- Thematic navigation (Tech, Energy, etc.)  
- Smart cross‑linking  
- Search bar quality  

Score Guide  
- 1 — No related content  
- 2 — Minimal suggestions  
- 3 — Basic related items  
- 4 — Strong cross‑linking  
- 5 — Excellent discovery ecosystem
