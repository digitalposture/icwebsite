Market Overview

Visualize sector and market distribution of investment certificates using an interactive sunburst chart.

How to use this tool

  1. Select a market snapshot date from the dropdown menu (defaults to Latest).
  2. Click the "Load Market Data" button to fetch and render the market breakdown.
  3. The sunburst chart will display hierarchical relationships across sectors and underlyings.
